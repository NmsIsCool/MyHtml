Youtube Music

Youtube Music is a music streaming service made by Google. Google used Javascript and Typescript for the frontend development and C, C++, Java, Go and Python for the backend

The databases behind YTM are Vitess, BigTable, and MariaDB

I use Youtube Music to play all my music at a high quality. Fun fact, Youtube Music may soon adopt a UI that is much more similar to its parent program, YouTube.
