Top 3 Apps

There are many apps spanning many platforms. But, I have 3 of my favorites. And they are:

  • Youtube Music - To play my Megadeth and Rammstein
  • Wrist Flashlight - This is a watch app. I use this to light my way while I struggle around my room at 3 am to find my water. But on my wrist.
  • GearPeriodicTable - Another watch app. I use this to brush up on my periodic table of elements. And to cheat on science tests
